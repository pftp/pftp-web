Hello!
You look like you want to learn to write code, and we want to guide you.
Why?
Because we think it's important that you understand the computing devices that are omnipresent in all of our lives.
Because the demand for quality computer scientists in this world is outpacing supply, and you can help bridge the gap.
And, most of all, because coding is a ton of fun once you get the hang of it.
FAQ, What Da
How much programming experience should I have coming into this course?

None. Nada. Zip. Zero. The course is designed to teach you from the ground up!

What will I actually learn from this course?

You will learn Python, our favorite programming language for just Getting Stuff Done. You'll start by drawing a bunch of things with turtle graphics, which is built into Python for learning purposes. About halfway through the course, we'll dive into realer world applications such as web scraping, data processing, and writing your own simple website.

Will you teach me to make my website beautiful?

Unfortunately, no. Though we'll touch on web development, we will NOT be teaching you how to make a nice-looking website, aka web design. For that, the folks over at The Web Design Workshop seem to know what they're doing.
